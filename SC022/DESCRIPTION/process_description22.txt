**Customer Inquiry and Order Placement Process** represents a customer service and sales workflow that guides prospective customers from their initial inquiry through product selection and order confirmation. The process begins when a customer submits an inquiry, triggering parallel activities to collect customer information and address questions or concerns. Once sufficient information has been gathered, the customer is guided in selecting an appropriate product or service, and a quotation is prepared and presented. Depending on the customer’s decision, the process may proceed directly to order placement or conclude without a purchase. When an order is placed, it is recorded in the system and an order confirmation is sent to the customer. The process may include additional interactions to refine customer requirements before a final decision is reached. The process concludes either when the customer chooses not to proceed or after the order has been successfully recorded and confirmed, completing the sales engagement cycle.
