This process represents a financial expense validation and payment workflow executed between the store and administrative departments. The process starts when the store registers a new expense request.

After the expense is registered, the administrative department validates the expense documentation and verifies whether the expense is valid or invalid. Based on the validation result, the process follows different execution paths.

If the expense is considered valid, the payment is executed and the process is successfully completed. If the expense is considered invalid, the payment is rejected and the store is informed about the payment denial before the process ends.

The workflow includes registration, validation, approval and rejection decisions, payment execution, and notification activities between organizational sectors.
