**Employee Recruitment and Onboarding Process** represents a human resources workflow that manages the complete lifecycle of hiring a new employee, from workforce demand identification to employee integration into the organization. The process begins with the recognition of a need for a new hire, followed by the creation and publication of a job description to attract candidates. Applications are collected, screened, and evaluated through initial phone interviews, after which candidates are invited to participate in either virtual or in-person interviews. Based on the interview outcomes, candidates may proceed through additional evaluation cycles until a suitable candidate is selected. The process then continues with offer extension, salary negotiation when required, and final hiring approval. Once the candidate accepts the offer, onboarding activities are initiated, including orientation, training, and completion of administrative documentation, which are performed in parallel. The process concludes after all onboarding requirements have been fulfilled and the new employee is fully integrated into the organization.
