This process represents a personal decision-making workflow related to Nico’s emotional state, needs, and leisure activities. The process begins when Nico feels bored. Based on his current condition and desires, different activity paths may be selected.

One possible path occurs when Nico becomes hungry, which leads him to eat a banana. Another possible path occurs when Nico develops an intense desire and watches funny videos. The process also includes a leisure alternative where Nico wants to go fishing and then proceeds to fishing activities.

Some activities are connected to entertainment resources represented through a data store. After completing one of the selected activities, the process converges into a final state where Nico becomes happy, concluding the workflow.
