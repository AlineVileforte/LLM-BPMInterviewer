**Independent Film Production** represents a complex entertainment production workflow that coordinates creative, technical, logistical, and promotional activities required to produce and distribute an independent film. The process begins with script approval, followed by parallel efforts involving casting preparation and location scouting. Casting activities lead to scene rehearsals and script detailing, while location activities trigger supplier hiring, soundtrack composition, stage construction, costume production, and filming equipment preparation. Depending on production needs, the workflow may include iterative cycles of additional filming before proceeding to the main filming phase. After filming, the process advances through visual effects production, preliminary editing, and post-production activities. In parallel, marketing campaigns and final sound editing are executed, while promotional material production and negotiations with film festivals may support movie distribution activities. The process concludes after all production, post-production, marketing, and distribution activities have been completed and synchronized, resulting in the finalized release of the independent film.
