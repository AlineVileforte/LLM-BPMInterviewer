**Complex Hospital Care Process** represents a complex hospital workflow that simultaneously coordinates clinical, administrative, and financial activities to ensure comprehensive patient care. The process begins with the patient's arrival and proceeds through triage, diagnosis, treatment, and monitoring stages, potentially involving additional examinations, specialist consultations, and hospitalization procedures. In parallel, administrative registration, insurance authorization, and cost management activities are performed. The process concludes after treatment completion, delivery of final patient instructions, and preparation of hospital discharge procedures.
