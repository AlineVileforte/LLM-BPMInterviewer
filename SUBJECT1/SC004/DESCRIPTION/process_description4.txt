This process represents a commercial sales workflow focused on handling customer offers, evaluating sales alternatives, and managing negotiations before completing a sale. The process includes activities related to collecting customer requests, reviewing offers, analyzing sales conditions, renegotiating proposals when necessary, and selecting the most appropriate commercial option. 

During the execution of the process, different sales paths may be followed depending on the customer’s needs and the evaluation of the available offers. The process also contains parallel coordination activities and subprocesses that support the management of negotiations and decision-making throughout the sales lifecycle.
