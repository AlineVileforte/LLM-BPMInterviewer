**Order Fulfillment and Delivery Process** represents an order management workflow that coordinates sales, fulfillment, logistics, and customer service activities from order placement to post-delivery support. The process begins when a customer places an order either online or by phone, followed by order confirmation and communication with the customer. In parallel, shipping documentation is prepared and ordered items are picked and packed for dispatch. Once these activities are completed, the order is handed over to a logistics provider, and tracking information is sent to the customer while shipment monitoring is performed. Depending on the delivery outcome, the process may continue with successful delivery confirmation and subsequent customer feedback or return-handling activities. The process concludes after all delivery, customer service, and post-purchase support activities have been completed, marking the end of the order fulfillment lifecycle.
