This process represents an emergency insurance assistance workflow used to evaluate insurance coverage requests and coordinate emergency support activities. The process begins when an emergency insurance request is received through a message event.

The organization first analyzes the insurance agreement to verify whether the requested emergency service is covered by the customer’s insurance policy. Based on the coverage analysis, the process follows different execution paths.

If coverage exists, the organization offers immediate help to the customer. After the assistance is provided, the organization evaluates whether the customer accepts or rejects the proposed support. If the customer accepts the support, the organization performs internal accounting activities. If the customer rejects the support, the organization requests a rejection notification.

If the insurance agreement does not provide coverage, the organization sends an offer for emergency help outside the insurance coverage conditions. Finally, after all administrative and decision-making activities are completed, the process ends.
