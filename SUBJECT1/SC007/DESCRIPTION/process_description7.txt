This process represents an integrated sales, logistics, accounting, and customer management workflow used to process customer orders and deliver goods. The process begins when a customer places an order, and the company checks whether the customer already exists in the system.

If the customer already exists, the company checks the customer’s payment history. Otherwise, the company checks the customer type and may create a new customer profile. Depending on the evaluation results, the process may send a declining message and terminate the order. If the customer is accepted, the company checks product availability.

When goods are unavailable, the missing goods are produced before the process continues. Once the goods are available, the company sends a confirmation to the customer and simultaneously prepares the delivery activities. The logistics department gets ready for delivery and packs the goods, while the accounting department prepares the invoice and sends it to the customer.
