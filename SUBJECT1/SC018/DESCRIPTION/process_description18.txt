This process represents an online cinema reservation workflow where customers access an information system to search for movie sessions and complete ticket reservations. The process starts when the customer connects to the information system and chooses the type of search to perform.

Depending on the selected search strategy, the customer may choose the movie, the screening room, or the screening time in different combinations. The workflow supports several navigation paths, including selecting the movie and time together, selecting the room and time together, or selecting the movie and room together.

After the customer completes the selection activities, the system generates a summary of the selected reservation details and verifies whether the customer is already registered in the system.

If the customer is already registered, the user performs login authentication before continuing with the reservation activity. If the customer is not registered, the process executes a registration subprocess where the customer provides nickname, password, and email information. The system validates the submitted data, may request data re-entry if validation fails, and stores the registration information in the database after successful verification.

Once the customer account is validated or authenticated, the reservation is completed, followed by the payment decision activity and the reservation confirmation with email notification.

After the cinema screening, the process also includes a customer satisfaction questionnaire workflow. Twenty-four hours after the movie session, the system sends a questionnaire email to the customer to collect feedback regarding the cinema experience.
