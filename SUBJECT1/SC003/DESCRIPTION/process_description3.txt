This process represents the validation and accounting flow of an expense payment within a store environment. The process starts when the sales department performs an expense request. After the expense is executed, the administrative department verifies the expense documentation to determine whether the submitted documents are valid and complete. 

If the documentation is approved, the payment is accounted for and the sales department receives a payment confirmation notification. If the documentation is not approved, the payment is not accounted for and the sales department receives a non-payment notification. 

The process includes validation, decision-making, accounting activities, and notification exchanges between the sales and administrative sectors.
