This process represents the execution of a property assessment procedure in which multiple verification activities are performed in parallel before the assessment can be completed. The process starts with the initiation of the assessment and then simultaneously executes three independent evaluation activities: checking the customer’s credit history, performing a property title search, and conducting a property survey.
The credit history verification evaluates the financial reliability of the customer, the property title search validates the legal ownership and possible restrictions related to the property, and the property survey analyzes the physical condition and characteristics of the property. Once all assessment activities are completed, the process concludes with the completion of the overall property assessment.
