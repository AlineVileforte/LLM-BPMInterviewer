This process represents an e-commerce checkout and payment workflow used by customers to purchase products online. The process begins when the customer pays the book or product fee and then selects or manages shipping information.

The customer may choose an existing shipping address, enter a new shipping address, or edit an existing one. After defining the address information, the process continues with the selection of shipping details, shipping preferences, and shipping speed.

The workflow also allows the customer to change quantities, delete items, or order existing gift items before proceeding to payment activities. Once these configurations are completed, the customer selects a payment method.

The payment can be performed using different alternatives such as credit or debit cards, Amazon.com Store Card, checking account, or gift cards and promotional codes. Depending on the selected payment option, the customer may switch currency or change the billing address before finalizing the purchase.

Finally, the customer places the order in the default currency, concluding the checkout process.
