**Incident Investigation and Corrective Action Process** represents an incident management workflow designed to investigate reported incidents, determine their root causes, implement corrective measures, and prevent future occurrences. The process begins with incident reporting, registration, and assignment to the appropriate team. Parallel activities are then performed to gather relevant information and identify the underlying cause of the incident. Based on the investigation findings, corrective actions are proposed and may include implementing technical fixes, conducting training, or modifying organizational policies. After the selected corrective measures are completed, follow-up activities are conducted to verify their effectiveness and ensure the issue has been properly addressed. The process concludes with the formal closure of the incident report and notification of all relevant stakeholders regarding the outcome and resolution of the incident.
