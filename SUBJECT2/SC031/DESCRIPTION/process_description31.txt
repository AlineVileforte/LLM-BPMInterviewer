**IT Solution Implementation and Support Process** represents an information technology service process that manages the evaluation, acquisition, deployment, and support of solutions requested by organizational departments. The process begins with the submission of an IT solution request, followed by parallel assessments of technical compatibility and cost and resource requirements. Depending on the identified needs, the process may include user training, procurement of tools or software licenses, and additional support activities. Once the required resources and approvals are in place, the solution is installed, tested, and deployed to the requesting department. After deployment, troubleshooting support may be provided when necessary to ensure successful adoption and operation. The process concludes when all implementation, deployment, and support activities have been completed and the requested solution is fully operational.
