**Marketing Campaign Management Process** represents a marketing workflow that plans, executes, monitors, and evaluates promotional campaigns to generate leads and support business growth. The process begins with the definition of campaign objectives, followed by parallel preparation activities including content creation, visual design, and the selection of promotional channels. Once all campaign assets are prepared, the campaign is launched, and parallel activities are performed to collect leads through customer relationship management systems and monitor campaign performance in real time. Depending on campaign outcomes, sales teams may follow up on generated leads while performance data is continuously analyzed. After the campaign period ends, performance evaluation and optimization activities are conducted to identify improvement opportunities for future initiatives. The process concludes when both the campaign execution and post-campaign analysis activities have been completed, providing insights to support future marketing strategies.
