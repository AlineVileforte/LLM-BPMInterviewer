**Inventory Replenishment and Stock Management Process** represents an inventory management workflow that monitors stock levels, replenishes inventory when necessary, and ensures that received goods are properly inspected, stored, and recorded. The process begins with a review of current inventory levels to determine whether replenishment actions are required. When stock shortages are identified, manual or automated alerts may be issued, triggering the procurement process. The workflow then proceeds with supplier ordering, updating expected delivery information, and receiving incoming stock. Upon receipt, parallel activities are performed to record inventory data and inspect the received goods for quality. Depending on storage requirements, approved stock may be placed either in storage areas or directly on shelves. After all receiving, inspection, storage, and recording activities are completed, inventory levels are updated to reflect the new stock position. The process concludes when the inventory records have been synchronized with the physical stock, ensuring accurate inventory availability information.
