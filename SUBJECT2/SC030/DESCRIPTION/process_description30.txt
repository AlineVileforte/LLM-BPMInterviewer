**Product Innovation and Prototype Development Process** represents a product development workflow that transforms an initial idea into a validated prototype ready for further development decisions. The process begins with the identification of a new product opportunity or improvement, followed by parallel research and feasibility analysis activities. Based on these findings, design concepts are created, evaluated, and used to select a promising solution, which is then developed into a prototype. Depending on the evaluation outcomes, the prototype may be refined iteratively before entering comprehensive testing. In parallel, functionality, safety, and market potential assessments are performed, and feedback from the testing phase is collected to support decision-making. The process concludes with either the approval of the prototype for further development or its rejection and disposal, marking the end of the innovation evaluation cycle.
