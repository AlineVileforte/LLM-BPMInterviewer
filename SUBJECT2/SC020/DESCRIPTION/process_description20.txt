This process represents a supply chain and order fulfillment workflow used to manage inventory availability, purchase order confirmation, product shipment, and invoice delivery activities. The process starts when a purchase order is received.

First, the organization checks the inventory status to determine whether the requested products are available. Based on the inventory verification result, the process follows two different execution paths.

If the inventory is not available, the purchase order is rejected and the process finishes without executing shipment or invoicing activities. If the inventory is available, the organization confirms the purchase order and proceeds with order fulfillment operations.

After the purchase order is confirmed, the workflow executes shipment and invoicing activities in parallel. One branch ships the product to the customer, while the other branch sends the invoice.

The process only concludes after both the shipment and invoice activities are completed, ensuring that the customer receives both the ordered product and the corresponding invoice.
