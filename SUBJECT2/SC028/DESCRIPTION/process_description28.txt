**Travel Ticket Booking and Customer Journey Process** represents a travel reservation workflow that manages the complete customer journey from ticket search and booking to post-travel services. The process begins when a customer searches for available tickets and, in parallel, selects a route and a preferred travel date and time. The customer then provides personal and payment information, enabling ticket generation. Following ticket issuance, parallel activities are performed to deliver the ticket through the selected communication channel and update seat inventory records. Depending on customer preferences and travel conditions, the process may include reminders, travel instructions, additional services, or post-travel feedback activities. After the customer completes the journey, follow-up services may be provided when applicable. The process concludes once all travel-related services and customer support activities have been completed, marking the end of the travel experience lifecycle.
