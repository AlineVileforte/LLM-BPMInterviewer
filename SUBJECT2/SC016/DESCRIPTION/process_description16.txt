This process represents an entertainment workflow for planning and attending a movie session at the cinema. The process begins when the customer accesses the Hoyts cinema website and searches for a movie that they like at a convenient schedule.

After selecting a movie, the process verifies whether seats are available for the desired session. If no seats are available, the process ends without purchasing tickets. If seats are available, the customer searches for discount cards to reduce the ticket price.

The workflow first checks whether the customer can use the “La Nacion” discount card. If the card is not found, the customer searches for a Citi discount card instead. Once a valid discount option is available, the customer pays for the tickets through the website.

After the payment is completed, the tickets are printed and the process waits until the movie time arrives. The customer then drives to the cinema, buys popcorn, gives the tickets to the ticket collector, enters the movie room, and sits down to watch the film.

The workflow concludes when the customer is seated in the cinema room, ready for the movie session.
