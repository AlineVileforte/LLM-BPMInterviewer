This process represents an academic exam enrollment workflow performed through the ESSE3 student system. The process starts when the student accesses the ESSE3 platform and enters the login credentials, including the login and password, followed by the confirmation action.

After authentication, the student navigates through the system by selecting the “Exams” item and then the “Exam Sessions” item. The student chooses the desired course and proceeds with the enrollment by pressing confirm multiple times without reviewing the displayed information. Once the enrollment is completed, the student can either print the enrollment confirmation or directly end the enrollment process.

After finishing the enrollment, the student hopes for a positive outcome regarding the exam participation. At this stage, the process may either end completely or continue with the enrollment in another exam, creating a repetition cycle that returns the process to the course selection activity.
