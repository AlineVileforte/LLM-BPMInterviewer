**Supplier Selection and Contracting Process** represents a procurement and supplier onboarding workflow that manages the identification, evaluation, selection, and engagement of suppliers to support organizational needs. The process begins with the recognition of a need for a new supplier or vendor, followed by the issuance of a request for proposals and the collection of supplier submissions. After proposals are received, they are evaluated, and additional assessment activities such as interviews or site visits may be conducted when required. Based on the evaluation outcomes, a supplier may be selected, and contract negotiations are initiated. The process then proceeds through contract signing, supplier onboarding, and contract execution activities to establish the business relationship. The process concludes once the supplier has been formally onboarded and the contractual agreement has been executed, enabling the supplier to begin providing the agreed products or services.
