**Land Application Investigation Process** represents a land administration workflow that performs a comprehensive assessment of a land application by coordinating multiple independent verification activities. The process begins with the initiation of an investigation and immediately launches parallel evaluations covering planned land actions, title administration records, native title status, parcel information, land valuation data, and survey plan registration records. Each verification activity independently examines a specific aspect of the land application to ensure legal, administrative, cadastral, and valuation requirements are satisfied. After all assessment activities have been completed, their results are consolidated and reviewed to produce a final investigation outcome. The process concludes with the formalization of the overall assessment result, providing a complete basis for land administration decision-making.
